Forest Fire Prediction

Welcome to our Forest Fire Prediction project! This advanced machine learning application uses regression techniques to predict the likelihood and intensity of forest fires based on various environmental factors.

About the Project

Forest fires are a significant environmental concern, causing extensive damage to ecosystems and posing risks to human life and property. Our project aims to leverage the power of machine learning to predict forest fire occurrences and their potential impact.

Key Features:

  • Utilizes regression algorithms to predict fire intensity
  • Analyzes multiple environmental factors such as temperature, humidity, wind speed, and rainfall
  • Provides valuable insights for fire prevention and management strategies
  • Helps in early warning systems and resource allocation for firefighting efforts

By accurately predicting forest fires, we can contribute to better forest management, reduce economic losses, and protect our valuable natural resources.
